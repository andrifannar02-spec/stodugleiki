    How to use it for teaching

    Start with baseline stability

    Set displacement, KM, and KG for the vessel. The app calculates initial GM and indicates whether the baseline condition is comfortable, marginal, or unstable.

    Demonstrate weight transfer

    Move weight upward to show how KG rises and GM falls. Move weight sideways to show the heeling moment and list angle.

    Try your own ship

    Enter your ship data and test deck cargo, ballast shifts, or off-center loading. Use the results as a learning aid, not as a replacement for approved stability documentation.
